My Favourite Games

  • Hollow Knight
  • Just Cause 2
  • Pyramida

A side-scroller roguelike where you play as a bug in an underground world that have been overtaken by a terrible disease.

This game stole several hours of my life, and I don't regret it at all.

This is by far my favourite game I've ever played, and the only issue I could find with it was that it didn't go on forever. Everything about this game is perfect, from the art, the music, and the story, to the gameplay mechanics and pacing.

The game was made by a small indie company and all the art was hand drawn by one man. The art, in my opinion, is one of the best parts of the game.

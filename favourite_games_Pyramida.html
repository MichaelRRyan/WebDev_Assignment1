My Favourite Games

  • Hollow Knight

  • Pyramida

A mini top-down survival management game where you command a small settlement of people and must keep them fed while defending them from skeletons.

A short indie game made by a small company of 4 people, Pyramida is an incredibly cute and fun game to keep you entertained for a few hours.

I'm not usually one for management games, but this one really got me into the genre. It's a short game, but has a small pricetag, and is well worth it.

The game features a brilliant calming musical score and very cute and simple art, the game is both entertaining and relaxing, a brilliant game to wind down to.

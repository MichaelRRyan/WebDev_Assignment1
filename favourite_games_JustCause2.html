My Favourite Games

  • Hollow Knight
  • Just Cause 2
  • Pyramida

A third person shooter game where you play as an agent with numerous interesting gadgets and weapons for fighting the enemy and causing chaos.

This was one of the first games I really sunk a lot of hours into. The game has so much to do, from exploring the large open world and clearing out enemy bases, to piloting high speed aircraft and taking part in races.

The game featured a lot of destructable environments and vehicles, blowing stuff up is always a lot of fun. It took me many months to actually complete the games story, as I spent more time messing around than playing the actual missions.
